my process

research

In every case of creating a new design it is incredibly important to do your research. In the case of branding you want to collect any and all information that you can on the business branding, brand identities, and your exact brand your client is aiming for.

brainstorming

For me brainstorming starts once all of the information I need is gathered. This consists of mind mapping, sketching, creating moodboards, and looking at various kinds of inspiration. This allows me to gather different perspectives, and evolve ideas into a design that is effective for the client.

concepting

Concepting is when a design starts to take off. Bysing all outcomes of research and brainstorming, I am able to come up with the visual considerations and the message communicated with any design. Creating a solid concept for branding whether it is in web or print is one of the most important steps in my process. Working with client I will be able to finalize the concept, making sure all aspects will be able to engage their target audience.

creating

This is when I will take any thumbs and comps that I have created and pull them together to create a final design. This is one of the most exciting parts of my design process as I’m able to take a web or brand design to a place that gives a client the identity that they’ve always wanted to communicate to their audience.
