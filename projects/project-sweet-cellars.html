sweet cellars

Sweet Cellars is an ice cream shop that specializes in making wine-flavored ice cream. To help increase their product sales, they needed to have an identity and package design to catch the eye of their audience. Sweet Cellars’ careful considerations in making their unique flavors appeals to creative women ages 21–30. These are women who often have a preference for high quality ice cream and wine, motivating them to crave a combination of the two.
