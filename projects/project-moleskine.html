moleskine

Moleskine has been a popular brand of notebooks, journals, and planners since 1997. They were seeking an ad campaign targeted towards young adults ages 21–30 that find use in Moleskine’s products. Within this audience there are many other specific individuals such as writers, artists, students, journalists, etc. Moleskine wanted an ad campaign that made it a point to target each of these individuals by having each ad within their campaign communicating to a specific Moleskin user.
