midwest nuclear research

Midwest Nuclear Research is one of the first Nebraska-based nuclear research facilities. Their goal is to provide education and to improve the health, well-being, and environment of mankind through the application of nuclear technology. MNR was interested in launching a website that would attract college students and educators from different fields, levels, and universities that have an interest in nuclear research.
