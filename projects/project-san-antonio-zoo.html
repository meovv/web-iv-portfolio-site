san antonio zoo

The San Antonio Zoo is the number one most visited Zoo in the United States. They follow their mission to foster appreciation and concern for all living things. The San Antonio Zoo is looking for an engaging 2013 Annual Report that focuses on what they provide to their visitors, as well as an update on their various animal collections and activities. The audience for their 2013 annual report are adults of the ages 30 and above. This audience consists of either employees of the San Antonio Zoo, volunteers, educators, sponsors, and donors with a strong appreciation of the hard work being put in to keep evolving the zoo.
