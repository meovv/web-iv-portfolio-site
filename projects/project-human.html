human

The LGBTQA+ Resource Center is a UNL-based center creating a space for people of all sexual orientations, gender identities and expression to feel included. Their Resource Center was wanting to have a social activism campaign shown on and around campus targeting towards UNL students ages 18–25. This campaign will make a push to try and stop targeted students from using hate words and other labels to describe another person based off of their sexual orientation, gender identity, or gender expression.
