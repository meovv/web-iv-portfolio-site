gorilla grocery

Gorilla Grocery is an organic grocery store located in the Midwest. They pride themselves on the quality of their products and the constant satisfaction of their customers. They were looking for a rebrand of their identity targeted towards young adults and families ages 21–30 that consider it a priority to shop for organic and high quality groceries. With the recent popularity of organic grocery stores, Gorilla Grocery wanted an identity that would stand out to their audience, showing their reliability.
